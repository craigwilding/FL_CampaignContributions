Florida Campaign Contributions

This project collects campaign contribution data pulled from the FL Campaign Finance Database

The data is used for:

  1. Count the amount of campaign contributions per district
  2. Identify the voter id of the campaign donors so candidates can use the information.

Contribution Data

I have added the campaign contributions of all FL House and Senate candidates in 2022.

The contribution data is stored in the folders:
  • DATA / State Races / 2022HD for 2022 FL State House District Races
  • DATA / State Races / 2022SD for 2022 FL State Senate District Races

Contribution Counts

The total contributions per districts are located in the CSV files below:

  • Results / State Races / 2022HD_Contrib_byDistrict.csv for 2022 FL State House District Races
  • Results / State Races / 2022SD_Contrib_byDistrict.csv for 2022 FL State Senate District Races

The contribution counts contain the following fields:

  1. District number
  2. DEM Candidate - Candidaet name*
  3. DEM Total - The total amount the campaign received
  4. DEM Party - The amount of campaign funds received from the candidate's party
  5. REP Candidate - Candidaet name*
  6. REP Total - The total amount the campaign received
  7. REP Party - The amount of campaign funds received from the candidate's party
  8. Overspend - The amount the candidate with the most money outspent the other
  9. District Total - The total amount raised by both candidates

    10. * An empty candidate name indicates no party candidate ran and the opponent ran unopposed in the general election. There may have been party challengers in the primary election.
